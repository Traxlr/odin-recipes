We makin tacos bois

This is what your taco meat will look like when you are done. cool.

This is a simple but great recipe to make some nice tacos on the fly!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs 85% lean ground beef
  • Soft taco shells
  • Seasoning packet from Old El Paso Taco kit
  • 1/3 Can of refried beans
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Water!

Steps

  1. Add a dash of oil to a non-stick pan
  2. Slap all the ground beef onto the pan
  3. Break beef up with spatula to small pieces and cook until brown on medium heat
  4. Pour off all the grease
  5. Add all of the taco seasoning packet to the tacos
  6. Add 2/3 cup of water
  7. Heat for a couple more minutes
  8. add 1/3 of a can of refried beans
  9. Break them up with spatula and leave on low heat
  10. Begin grating cheese
  11. Microwave soft shells to warm
  12. Plate cooked taco meat on shells, add cheese!
